MEDICAL CODING ISN’T EVERYONE’S CUP OF TEA

Professional medical coding can make or break your medical practice. Keeping updated with the latest medical coding procedures will mitigate the claims denials. However, most medical organizations assign healthcare coding to the in-house medical coding team. They are already occupied with other administrative tasks, which affects their performance. Keeping an in-house medical coding team is costly in terms of money and time. For maximum efficiency, you need to certify professional medical coders who can play their role in accurate medical codes.

CPT Codes

Brisk Services uses CPT codes to communicate with healthcare practitioners and insurance companies effectively.

ICD-10 codes

Brisk Services follows ICD-10 codes which are directly maintained by the World Health Organization to ensure correct diagnosis.
